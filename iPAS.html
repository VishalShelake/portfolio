iPAS
The story behind app
This application is for District Collectors, District Planning Officers, and All Implementation Agency Users who are members of the District Planning Committee (DPC). Users can submit work inspections and expenditure data for works allocated to them by the District Planning Committee using this Application Implementation. This application uses geo-tagging on images captured by this application, and the District Planning Committee User may do supervision on the work inspection supplied by the Implementation Agency.
